# Budget Tracker

A simple personal Budget Tracker to keep track of your expenses and budget.

## Features
- Set your starting budget
- Add and categorize expenses
- View total expenses and remaining balance
- Visualize budget usage with a progress circle

## Usage
1. Enter your starting budget and click "Set Budget".
2. Add expenses by filling out the form (name, amount, category, date) and click "Add Expense".
3. See your total expenses, remaining balance, and budget utilization update automatically.
